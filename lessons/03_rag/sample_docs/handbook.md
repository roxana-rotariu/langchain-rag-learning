# Acme Corp — Employee Handbook (sample)

## Working hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. Employees may shift
their start time between 7:00 and 10:00 with manager approval (flexible hours).

## Remote work
Employees can work remotely up to three days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director approval and are reviewed every six months.

## Vacation
Full-time employees accrue 25 days of paid vacation per year. Up to 5 unused days
may be carried over into the following year; the rest expire on December 31.

## Equipment
Each new employee receives a laptop and a 27-inch monitor. Hardware is replaced
every four years, or sooner if it fails. Personal use of company laptops is allowed
within reason.

## Expenses
Work-related expenses under 100 EUR can be approved by a direct manager. Anything
above 100 EUR requires finance department approval and an itemized receipt.

## Security
Passwords must be at least 12 characters and rotated every 90 days. Multi-factor
authentication is mandatory for all internal systems.
